Community Guidelines

Last Updated: January 2025

DaeSoop is a safe space for sharing your thoughts anonymously. To ensure all users can comfortably share their stories, please follow the community guidelines below.

Zero Tolerance Policy

DaeSoop enforces a Zero Tolerance Policy. If any prohibited behavior is identified, the content will be immediately removed without prior warning, and severe violations may result in permanent account suspension.

1. Prohibited Behavior

1.1 Violence and Hate Speech

  • Profanity, vulgar language, or insulting expressions
  • Hate speech targeting specific individuals or groups
  • Discriminatory expressions based on race, gender, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, disability, etc.
  • Content that promotes or threatens violence
  • Content that encourages self-harm or suicide

1.2 Pornography and Sexual Content

  • Pornography or sexually explicit content
  • Inappropriate content involving minors
  • Sexual harassment or abuse
  • Non-consensual intimate images

1.3 Privacy Violations

  • Exposing others' personal information (name, contact, address, school, workplace, etc.)
  • Posting photos or videos of others without consent
  • Doxxing or cyberstalking
  • Distributing information that can identify others

1.4 Fraud and Spam

  • Advertising, promotions, or spam content
  • Phishing, scams, or monetary solicitation
  • Distribution of malicious links or viruses
  • Promotion of gambling, illegal drugs, or illegal services

1.5 Illegal Activities

  • Content that encourages or solicits illegal activities
  • Copyright-infringing content
  • Spreading false information or fake news
  • Defamation or spreading false statements

1.6 Service Abuse

  • Creating and abusing multiple accounts
  • Activities using automated tools (bots)
  • Actions that interfere with service operations
  • Abuse of the reporting system

2. Sanctions

The following actions will be taken when violations are confirmed:

  • Immediate Content Removal: Violating posts/comments will be removed without prior notice.
  • Temporary Account Suspension: 7-30 days suspension for repeated violations
  • Permanent Account Suspension: For severe violations or 3+ repeated offenses
  • Legal Action: Illegal activities may be reported to authorities.

Note: To appeal a sanction, please contact us using the information below.

3. How to Report

Found inappropriate content? Please report it using the following method:

  1. Tap the More (...) button on the post or comment.
  2. Select the "Report" option.
  3. Choose the reason for reporting and submit.

24-Hour Review Promise

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours of receipt. Severe violations (violent threats, content involving minors, etc.) are prioritized.

4. Blocking Users

If you don't want to see content from a specific user, you can block them:

  1. Tap the More (...) button on the post.
  2. Select "Block this user".
  3. Posts and comments from the blocked user will no longer be displayed.

5. Recommendations for a Safe Community

  • Use respectful and considerate language.
  • Write responsibly, even when anonymous.
  • Do not share personal information (including your own).
  • Actively report inappropriate content.
  • If you're going through a difficult time, please use professional counseling services.
